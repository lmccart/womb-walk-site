Womb Walk

Will you be my imaginary baby? I wear a pregnant belly prosthetic under my clothes. I walk around the city speaking to my belly, describing what I see around me as I move. You remotely join the walk in the role of the baby, listening to my voice. You control my movements by triggering small internal kicks to the sides of my belly directing me when to turn. Together, we navigate the city, with imagined baby as interface.
